Research Compendium

  1. Organize files according to a prevailing convention.

  2. Provide separation between data, methods and results expressing unambiguously the relationship between the three.

  3. Specify the environment (+ reproducibility).

Why is there no career for software developers in academia?

  • In 2012, the Software Sustainability Institute (SSI) organized the Collaborations Workshop that addressed this question.

  • The work of the participants and of the SSI’s policy team led to the foundation of the UK RSE association and later to the Society of Research Software Engineering

{Definition}: reseach software engineer

What do RSEs do?

Fundamentally, RSEs build software to support scientific research. They generally don’t have research questions of their own — they develop the computer tools to help other people to do cool things.

“I don’t know very much about these particular research fields, so I work closely with the oncologists or neuroscientists or whomever to develop the software that’s needed.”

Woolston (2022) Why science needs more research software engineers - Nature career

URSSI’s mission is to improve the recognition , development, and use , of software for a more sustainable research enterprise.

What is a Research software engineer?

Research Software Engineers use expertise in programming to advance research. This includes researchers who spend a significant amount of time programming, full-time software engineers writing code to solve research problems, and those somewhere in-between.
